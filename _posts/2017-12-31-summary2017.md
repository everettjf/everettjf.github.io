Just like that, 2017 is over. Every year is an important year, and this year was the first full year (calendar year, January to December) I worked at Alibaba (Ant Financial). The novelty and discomfort I [wrote about in last year's review](https://everettjf.github.io/2016/12/25/join-ant-financial-3-month-and-summary-for-2016/) have all become routine and familiar by now.

<!-- more -->

# Plan Completion


[Last year's plan is here](https://github.com/everettjf/everettjf.github.io/blob/master/stuff/plan/plan2017.png). If I'm lenient with myself, the completion can be called a barely-passing grade. Goals I didn't achieve:

1. Didn't travel abroad with my family. (Various reasons — not enough vacation days — and in the end I didn't go.)
2. Didn't join a franchise. (Later I thought I was too naive, or didn't dare to break out.)
3. Couldn't communicate normally in spoken English. (Still didn't put in enough time.)
4. Write a 200-page book. (I felt I still hadn't accumulated enough.)

What I achieved or did:

1. The rental can be considered a calm, deliberate search. Very close to the kindergarten.
2. Stocks, through Alibaba and JD stocks, exceeded 1000 RMB. (Still at a super-beginner stage; the stock rise was purely due to the macro environment.)
3. Read a big pile of finance books.
4. Attended two company financial management events.
5. Read some books about franchises. (Counts as getting acquainted, I guess.)
6. Spoken English improved significantly; still keeping up with practice on Liulishuo and Youdao Speaking. (Just haven't broken through the final stage yet.)
7. Developed the first beta version of [MachOExplorer](https://github.com/everettjf/MachOExplorer). (With very limited working time, developed it on and off; most of the code was written between midnight and 2 AM.)
8. Developed [AppleTrace](https://github.com/everettjf/AppleTrace) to solve the problem of analyzing app performance at work, but couldn't squeeze out time at work to develop it.
9. One blog post per month — basically just enough to fill the quota, but the quality was clearly worse than last year. (Still didn't make the blog a priority.)
10. AzeriaLab's arm exploitation practice. (Returning to security — this is where my interest lies.)
11. The direction of study leaned mostly toward low-level stuff; read some books and papers, mostly summarized in these two places, [iosre](http://iosre.com/t/topic/4680) and [papers](https://github.com/everettjf/papers). (On this I didn't stray off course.)
12. Quite a lot of self-driving trips in and around Beijing; played through all the family-friendly places.
12. Initial pwnable.kr practice. (As a closing note, here's a picture.)

# Habits Formed

1. This year I developed a more effective note-taking habit. Here's a summary of my note-taking scheme:
    
    ```
	1. OneNote笔记、日记、博客草稿。
	2. Pocket 乱七八糟的网页、各类感觉不错但没时间或者以后永远不看的文章。

	4. MWeb 写博客、电子书。
	5. dynalist.io 思维整理。
    ```

2. Quit Honor of Kings. (I have to say, this game is very popular, but to quit something, you have to make the probability of it happening smaller. By dealing with all the runes, going AFK multiple times, getting banned multiple times, in the end I lost interest in playing.)
3. Wake up at 7:20, sleep before 1 AM. (7 hours of sleep basically guaranteed, but sometimes 7 hours feels not enough.)

# Work Summary

A full year of work at Ant let me feel the power of collaboration. Developing Alipay mini-programs involved hundreds of people; such a huge project going from zero to release relied on the effort of every link in the chain. Following Alipay App's performance for a whole year also gave me a deeper understanding of future directions. Sweet, sour, bitter, spicy — hard to put into words (and not really appropriate to publish publicly) — but plenty of growth and gains too.

# Looking Ahead to 2018

5. Polish MachOExplorer, implement Windows support, and reach the goal of fully replacing MachOView.
6. Pair AppleTrace with frida to enhance the trace experience.
7. Finish the 200-page book before July 1 (free open-source or published — to be decided depending on the finished quality).
8. Travel abroad with the family once.
9. Add more images to blog posts; get hands-on with Sketch.
10. Have the blog cover more low-level principles and discoveries.
11. Achieve normal communication in spoken English.

# 工作总结

一整年的蚂蚁工作，让我体会到了协作的力量，支付宝小程序的开发涉及上百人，如此庞大的工程从零到发布，少不了每个环节的努力。跟进了一整年支付宝App的性能，也让我对未来的方向有更深入的认识。酸甜苦辣，一言难尽（也不太适宜公开发表），成长和收获也是多多。
